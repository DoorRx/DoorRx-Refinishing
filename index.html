About Us

Welcome to Door Rx Refinishing! We are Arizona's premier, on-site, door refinishing company.
First Impressions, Crafted Daily is more than our slogan—it's our promise. We specialize in bringing new life to entry doors, wood doors, iron doors, and cabinets, using the highest-quality materials and craftsmanship.
Serving Chandler and the greater Phoenix area, our team is committed to excellence and customer satisfaction. Let us help your property make the best first impression!

Our Services

  • Door Refinishing: Restore and protect your wood or iron doors with professional finishes.
  • Door Repair: Fix damage, cracks, or wear on entry doors for lasting beauty and durability.
  • Door Painting & Staining: Custom colors and stains to match your vision.
  • Cabinet Refinishing: Update your kitchen or bathroom cabinets with expert refinishing and painting.
  • On-Site Service: All work done at your home or business—no need to remove your doors!
